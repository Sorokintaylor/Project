About PewDiePie

Felix Arvid Ulf Kjellberg, known online as PewDiePie, is a Swedish YouTuber and comedian.

Kjellberg's channel became the most-subscribed on YouTube on August 15th 2013, but it currently holds the title of the second-most subscribed, his channel has over 108 million subscribers and has received over 26 billion views.

His favorite quote is “Don't be yourself, be a pizza. Everyone loves pizza.”
